FEATURES OF THE OBJECT

1

Safety

The building is based on the barretta method.

The baretta is a reinforced cage that sinks more than 50m into the ground and is filled with concrete. The system has an increased load-bearing capacity and is used in the construction of skyscrapers (e.g. in Dubai, Oman, etc.).

2

Advanced fire protection system

  • smoke detectors.
  • fire resistant entrance doors in each residence.
  • sprinklers.
  • effective smoke extraction system in the corridors.
